About Our Application

Our Product Expiry Notification System helps businesses manage inventory by tracking product expiration dates and sending timely notifications to prevent losses and ensure compliance with safety standards.

Our Features

  • Notifications: Sends alerts to users about upcoming expirations via email and in-app messages.
  • Inventory Management: Provides tools to manage stock levels and product details efficiently.
